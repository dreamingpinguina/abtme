Who Am I? 我是谁？

I'm a video producer at italki (2023-now), handling script writing, storyboard creation, video editing, and MG animation design for YouTube and TikTok ads. 我目前在 italki 担任视频制作人 (2023至今)，主要负责YouTube和TikTok广告的脚本撰写、分镜设计、视频剪辑以及MG动画制作。

I love pop culture and often participate in promotional and photography activities for stage arts. With extensive experience in video studios and MCN organizations, I've worked on content for brands like Sweaty Betty, Kao, and Dyson. 我热爱流行文化，经常参与舞台艺术的宣传和摄影活动。我拥有丰富的视频工作室和MCN机构工作经验，曾为 Sweaty Betty, Kao (花王), Dyson 等品牌制作内容。

I have collaborated with influencers such as 花小雕, 郑鑫Echo, and Yawning Lion. A documentary I worked on, "Mi Nombre Es...", was shortlisted by the Spanish Equality Department Women's Association. 我曾与 花小雕, 郑鑫Echo, 和 Yawning Lion 等知名博主合作。我参与制作的纪录片 "Mi Nombre Es..." 曾入围西班牙平等部妇女协会奖项。

Since 2021, I’ve also been running a small channel with friends, where we discuss a variety of topics in a lighthearted way. I currently volunteer with some grassroots social organizations, focusing on social media communications and street interviews. 2021年起和朋友一起开始做自己的小频道，侃天侃地，严肃不正经。目前，我也在一些民间组织做志愿者，专注社交媒体传播和街头采访。
